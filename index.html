About NSCC Rideshare

NSCC Ridehsare is a NSCC exclusive app for students to both get in contact with each oher and help plan carpool rides to get to theri campuses Using the app. students can create routes from their place of residence to their campuss and student drivers can check for route and plan with each other to pick up those in need to get to college. Allow to decide meetup spots, plan for multipple pickups and alert students of ongoing weather and incidents on he road to help get there safely
